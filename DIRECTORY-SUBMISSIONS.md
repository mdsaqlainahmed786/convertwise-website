## LONG DESCRIPTION (under 1000 chars)
```
Nimitai is an AI meeting intelligence and conversation intelligence platform built exclusively for B2B SaaS sales teams, founders, and early-stage sales organizations.

Unlike basic call recorders, Nimitai uses three specialized AI agents that cover the entire sales cycle:

Audit Agent: Analyzes your current pitch deck, demo flow, and past meeting recordings to identify weak points and optimization opportunities before you even book the call.

Prep Agent: Pulls every shred of intel on your prospect and compiles it into a 90-second battle card with talking points, company signals, and key insights.

Live Agent: Sits in your meeting window during active calls, analyzing facial expressions and engagement signals, alerting you to perfect conversion moments as they happen.

The platform tracks buyer emotion, purchase intent, and engagement patterns automatically — surfacing real-time coaching prompts so reps always know when to lean in, pivot, or close.

Built as a Gong alternative at $149/seat/month. Private beta with 48-hour onboarding.
```
