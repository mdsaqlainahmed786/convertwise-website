Color Rules

Do
  • Use dark BG + orange for all external marketing and social
  • Use light BG + indigo/purple for in-app product screens
  • Orange (#E89422) bridges both — CTAs on marketing, alerts in product
  • Green for success/wins in both contexts
  • Purple charts in product, orange highlights in marketing
Never
  • Blue/purple in social media posts or marketing (that's product only)
  • Dark midnight backgrounds inside the product dashboard
  • Pure black (#000000) anywhere — use #070B18 or #1E293B
  • Mix both palettes randomly in the same asset
  • Use light/pastel colors for external marketing content

04 — Voice & Tone

How Nimitai Speaks

Direct. Data-driven. Founder-led. Same voice in marketing copy and product microcopy — just adapted intensity.

We say
  • "Record every call. Analyze every signal. Coach every rep."
  • "Your close rate goes up 31% in two months."
  • "Gong charges $28K/year. We charge $149/seat/month."
  • "That deal died in the first 15 minutes of your demo."
  • "Built by founders, for founders."
  • "Apply for founding access."
We never say
  • "Our cutting-edge solution leverages AI to..."
  • "We are delighted to announce..."
  • "Best-in-class conversation intelligence..."
  • "Synergize your sales workflow..."
  • "Contact our sales team for a custom quote."
  • "Sign up for a free trial."

Voice Across Contexts

Marketing Voice (High Intensity)

"Sales teams miss 70% of buying signals buried in call recordings."

Bold claims. Sharp contrast. Competitive edge. Urgency to act.

Product Voice (Calm + Confident)

"Your confidence is up 12% this week. You're crushing it!"

Encouraging. Data-backed. Friendly. Helps reps feel prepared, not pressured.

Copy Formulas

Pattern: Pain → Proof → CTA
  • Pain: "Sales teams miss 70% of buying signals buried in call recordings."
  • Proof: "Nimitai detects them in real time — 87% accuracy across 18 demos."
  • CTA: "Apply for founding access. $149/seat/month."
Pattern: Competitor → Contrast → Close
  • Competitor: "Gong charges $28,000/year for 5 seats."
  • Contrast: "Nimitai gives you the same AI coaching for $149/seat/month. Live in 48 hours."
  • Close: "Which one would you pick?"

06 — Social Strategy

Channel Playbook

Each platform has different rules. Same brand voice, adapted format. Social always uses the marketing palette (dark + orange).

LinkedIn (Primary Channel)

Founder-Led Growth Engine

3-4 posts/week from Nilansh + Archit's personal accounts. Company page reposts.

  • Mon: Sales insight / hot take (text post)
  • Wed: Product dashboard screenshot or demo clip (video/carousel)
  • Thu: Founder journey / building-in-public (text post)
  • Sat: Competitor comparison or data insight (carousel)
Instagram

Visual Brand + Behind the Scenes

Feed: Dark-themed brand graphics with product UI highlights. Stories: Raw founder content. Reels: Quick sales tips.

  • Feed: 3 posts/week — dark BG carousels with orange accents + product screenshots
  • Stories: Daily — office life, product updates, polls
  • Reels: 2/week — 15-30s sales tips, product demos
  • Highlights: Product, Pricing, Results, Team, FAQ
X / Twitter

Hot Takes + Threads

Short, punchy takes on sales, AI, and SaaS. Weekly threads with deep insights.

  • Daily: 1-2 tweets — sales hot takes, product updates
  • Weekly: 1 thread — deep dive (Gong pricing, sales tactics)
  • Format: Short sentences. No fluff. Hook in first line.
  • Engage: Reply to sales leaders, VCs, SaaS founders
YouTube

Demo + Education

Product walkthroughs showing actual dashboard, sales coaching content, competitor comparisons.

  • Weekly: 1 video — product dashboard walkthrough or sales tutorial
  • Shorts: 2/week — quick sales tips from Reels
  • SEO titles: "Gong vs Nimitai", "Best Sales Call Software 2026"
  • Thumbnails: Dark BG, orange text, face shots + product UI peek

Instagram Post Design Rules

Design System
  • Background: Always #070B18 (Midnight) for graphics
  • Headline text: White (#FFFFFF), Inter Bold
  • Accent text/numbers: #E89422 or #F5B040
  • Product screenshots on light BG are OK as carousel slides
  • Nimitai logo bottom-right, small
  • Max 2 fonts per post (heading + body)
Never
  • All-white/light backgrounds for brand graphics (screenshots are OK)
  • Stock photos of people shaking hands
  • More than 3 colors per post
  • Decorative fonts or script typefaces
  • Clip art or generic icons
  • Walls of text — max 6 words per line
